Highlights of Lamoni, Iowa



Graceland University is the main attraction in Lamoni because it is a college and everyone love to go on college visits. It lies in the middle of the town where a lot of alumni and current students live. It is a liberal arts college that cares about the people who come here and work here. GU show their appreciation through various of events that spotlight the students and workers talent. Hosting events like Scholar Showcase for students and workers appreciation days by having BBQ's, parties, or by leaving comments around school showing that they care.


Graceland coliseum is where all the backetball, volleyball, and indoor track and field take place. Its where people get together to play IM games and for hosting prizes for Final Fling or other events.
